Dawat-e-Islami

Dawat-e-Islami is a non-political Islamic organization based in Pakistan, founded by Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri in 1981. It aims to propagate the teachings of Islam based on the Quran and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). The organization operates globally and is known for its emphasis on spiritual development, religious education, and community service. Dawat-e-Islami uses various mediums such as literature, online resources, and organized events to reach out to Muslims worldwide. It focuses on fostering a deeper understanding of Islamic principles and practices among its followers. The organization promotes peace, tolerance, and unity among Muslims, advocating a balanced approach to religious observance in contemporary society. Propagation of Islam:

  • Dawat-e-Islami focuses on spreading the message of Islam as derived from the Quran and the Sunnah. They emphasize the importance of following the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) in everyday life.


Educational Initiatives:

  • The organization provides educational resources and programs to enhance religious knowledge among Muslims. This includes publishing books, pamphlets, and online content that cover a wide range of Islamic topics.


Islamic Outreach and Education:

  • This involves organizing events, seminars, and workshops to educate both Muslims and non-Muslims about Islamic beliefs, practices, and values.


Community Service:

  • Dawat-e-Islami encourages its members to engage in charitable activities and community service. This includes helping the needy, supporting local communities, and promoting social welfare initiatives.


Media and Publications:

  • The organization uses modern media platforms like television, radio, and the internet to reach a wider audience. They produce literature, videos, and online content to spread Islamic teachings and address misconceptions.

Dawat-e-Islami is known for its adherence to Sunni Islam and its emphasis on following the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah (traditions of the Prophet Muhammad). Members of Dawat-e-Islami, known as "Mureeds," participate actively in the organization's activities and contribute to its mission of promoting Islamic values and teachings.

Dawat-e-Islami is a global Islamic organization dedicated to propagating the teachings of Islam through peaceful preaching and educational outreach. With a commitment to social welfare, Dawat-e-Islami actively participates in humanitarian efforts, supporting the less fortunate and advocating for justice and equality.
